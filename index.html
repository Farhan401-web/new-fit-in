About the Owner

The co-founders of FitIn are,

Sagar Gugaliya , he is a seasoned entrepreneur who started Shreya’s Enterprises Mobile Shop in 2005 with just one display counter on Dehuroad’s bustling Main Bazar. Two decades later, the store has grown into a trusted destination for smartphones, accessories, and after-sales service. Driven by his passion for health, Sagar diversified in 2017 by launching Fit In Fitness Club—a modern facility offering cardio, strength training, yoga, Zumba, Bollywood dance, CrossFit, and circuit sessions. His dual ventures embody his motto: “Technology in your hand, fitness in your life.”

Parvez Shaikh, he is a passionate fitness professional with an impressive journey that began in 2007. With nearly two decades of experience in the fitness industry, Parvez has trained, guided, and inspired hundreds of individuals to lead healthier lives. In 2017, he teamed up with his childhood friend Dilawar Khan and entrepreneur Sagar Googliya to co-found Fit In Fitness Club—a fully equipped, community-driven gym located in Vikas Nagar, Pendse Colony, Dehuroad. Parvez continues to play a hands-on role in day-to-day gym operations, fitness programming, and client transformation

Dilawar Khan,he is a dynamic individual who brings the perfect balance of corporate professionalism and entrepreneurial passion. A skilled IT Manager by profession, Dilawar has always believed in the power of fitness to transform lives. In 2017, driven by a shared vision and deep-rooted friendship, he joined hands with Parvez Shaikh and Sagar Googliya—his childhood friends—to co-found Fit In Fitness Club in Dehuroad’s Vikas Nagar. Though his weekdays are spent managing tech teams, his heart lies in promoting fitness as a lifestyle and supporting the gym’s long-term growth.
